Philosophy Club

Learn about the 10 most common schools of thought in philosophy and the most famous supporters of each. Our Club meets bi-weekly on Thursday evenings at 6pm at the campus cafe. We encourage discussion of other philosophies and philosphers not listed in the table below as well. Of course, we expect our members to maintain respect and dignity for all even when debate can become empassioned, but pughing these bounds of thoughts and decorum, within reason, can lead to some profound breakthroughs in thought! Our meetings are always fun and guaranteed to stimulate. Click on join to fill out the registration form. Hope to see you there!
